Why Framer Is the Perfect Tool for Designer

Framer combines design and development, allowing you to build interactive websites without deep coding knowledge. With its visual editor, responsive grid system, and React support, you can bring any idea to life — from portfolios to landing pages. Beyond static layouts, Framer empowers designers to prototype real interactions, animations, and transitions with pixel-perfect precision. Its built-in CMS and seamless publishing tools make it easy to launch and iterate without relying on developers. Whether you're crafting a personal brand or building a client project, Framer offers the creative freedom and technical power to go from concept to live site — all in one place.

Key Features That Make Your Website Shin

Hover effects, animations, custom components — all available directly in the browser. Framer also includes built-in hosting, SEO optimization, and fast loading, making it ideal for designers, marketers, and startups.

From Design to Deployment in Minutes

Once your design is ready, simply hit “Publish” and your site goes live instantly. No need to configure servers or domains manually. Framer automatically generates SSL certificates, optimizes performance, and ensures smooth experience across all devices.
